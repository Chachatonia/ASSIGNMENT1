THE FLUFFIEST PANCAKES YOU WILL EVER MAKE!

This is the easiest recipe on how to make the fluffiest pancakes on earth, serving 3 to 4 people

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 2 eggs
  • half a cup milk
  • a pinch of salt
  • 1 cup melted butter
  • two table spoons of sugar
  • a teaspoon cinnamon
  • your choice of toppings eg; yoghurt, chocolate chips,honey or berries

    • PROCEDURE

    Mix the dry ingredients by adding the flour, sugar, salt and cinamon in a large bowl.

    Separate the egg yolks from the whites and whisk the whites till fluffy

    In a second smaller bowl, add milk, melted butter, egg yolks and whisk thoroughly

    Pour in the contents of the smaller bowl into the larger bowl and whisk till smooth

    Once all lumps dissappear,add the egg whites and add your fruit or topping of choice

    Mix gently till well combined

    In a hot pan melt one teaspoon of butter and add a cup of your batter

    Once the lower part is cooked flip the pancake onto the upper side

    Once ready,sprinkle toppings of your choice.

    Serve with your choice of beverage
